# 02 RULE AND WORKFLOW REGISTER

This register inventories the rules and workflows observed in the reviewed sources.

## Workflows

### WF-01: Product Definition Workflow
*   **Title:** گردش کار جامع و تکامل‌پذیر فرآیند تعریف کالا در شاپفا
*   **Source Path:** `اسناد/ARIA-G-Product-Definition-Process-Workflow.md`
*   **Applicable Roles:** R11, R1, R2, R4, R7, R9
*   **Trigger:** Receipt of raw product lists [INFERRED: or task cards].
*   **Required Evidence/Input:** [INFERRED: Product name, brand, basic unverified features.]
*   **Expected Output:** Finalized JSON payloads [INFERRED: and XLSX files ready for Shopfa import].
*   **Blocking Condition:** Validation errors return the workflow to Step 1. [INFERRED: Failure at the R7 step routes the task back to R11.]
*   **Explicit Workflow Rules:**
    1. Short description length: 120 to 160 characters.
    2. Tags: 3 to 5 strategic keywords.
    3. Attribute uniformity for each product category.
    4. Validation errors return the workflow to Step 1.
    5. Shopfa operator changes require local archive update through `sync_product_archive.py`.
*   **Unresolved Ambiguity:** The exact mechanical trigger that transitions the task from step 4 (SEO) to step 5 (Validation) is implicitly handled by R0 rather than explicitly defined in the document.

### WF-02: Product Publishing & Sync Workflow
*   **Title:** Product Publishing Workflow / Product Sync Protocol
*   **Source Path:** `اسناد/ARIA-G-Product-Publishing-Workflow.md` & `اسناد/ARIA-G-Product-Sync-Protocol.md`
*   **Applicable Roles:** Human Operator, R9, R0
*   **Trigger:** Successful completion of WF-01.
*   **Required Evidence/Input:** Validated JSON payloads.
*   **Expected Output:** Live Shopfa products, updated `بایگانی/` files.
*   **Blocking Condition:** Shopfa dashboard constraints or attribute mismatches block the sync.
*   **Unresolved Ambiguity:** Manual intervention by a human operator is required for actual upload, creating a gap in fully autonomous execution.

## Rules

### RL-01: Anti-Leakage Search Rules
*   **Title:** پیشگیری از نشت اطلاعاتی و تشابه اسمی (Anti-Leakage Search Rules)
*   **Source Path:** `.agents/AGENTS.md`
*   **Applicable Roles:** R11
*   **Trigger:** Any search engine query generated by R11.
*   **Required Evidence/Input:** Target keyword or brand name.
*   **Expected Output:** Search queries containing negative filters (`-`) and limited to specific Iranian stationery domains.
*   **Blocking Condition:** Queries without negative filters are invalid.
*   **Unresolved Ambiguity:** No automated mechanism exists to enforce or reject non-compliant queries before execution.

### RL-02: Competitor Monitoring Focus
*   **Title:** پایش و تحلیل ۳ رقیب برتر بازار ایران
*   **Source Path:** `.agents/AGENTS.md`
*   **Applicable Roles:** R11
*   **Trigger:** Market research and specification gathering.
*   **Required Evidence/Input:** Target product.
*   **Expected Output:** Extracted data strictly from Digikala, Braitore/Iran Tahrir, and Torob/Emalls.
*   **Blocking Condition:** Sourcing data from unapproved competitors.
*   **Unresolved Ambiguity:** Action to take if a product is entirely absent from all three approved competitors is undefined.

### RL-03: Fact Registration Rule
*   **Title:** قانون ثبت فکت‌ها
*   **Source Path:** `اسناد/ARIA-G-Product-Definition-Process-Workflow.md`
*   **Applicable Roles:** R11, R1
*   **Trigger:** Definition of any new product.
*   **Required Evidence/Input:** Verified product specs.
*   **Expected Output:** Updated `known_facts.json`.
*   **Blocking Condition:** A product cannot be defined without prior registration in `known_facts.json`.
*   **Unresolved Ambiguity:** The physical location or central authority of `known_facts.json` is not clearly established across all workspaces.

### RL-04: SKU Generation Formula
*   **Title:** تخصیص ساختار و کدگذاری SKU عددی
*   **Source Path:** `اسناد/ARIA-G-Product-Definition-Process-Workflow.md` & `اسناد/ARIA-G-SKU-Architecture-Standard.md`
*   **Applicable Roles:** R11, R4
*   **Trigger:** Initial product structuring.
*   **Required Evidence/Input:** Brand code, Category code, Model number, Variant code.
*   **Expected Output:** Concatenated numeric SKU (BRN+CAT+MDL+VAR).
*   **Blocking Condition:** Missing or alphanumeric components.
*   **Unresolved Ambiguity:** None.
