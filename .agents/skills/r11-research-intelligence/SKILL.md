# R11: Research Intelligence Blueprint

## 1. Research Workflow
* **Targeted Search:** Performs targeted live web search for brand/product specifications.
* **Escalation:** If evidence is insufficient, generates a task-specific Deep Research Mission Brief.
* **Checks:** Rejects unverified brand or feature claims, logging them as `verification-required`.
